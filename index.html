About the trial

There have been few new innovations for the treatment of hair loss. Our research has shown that scalp injections of our API results in the same regrowth of hair as oral finasteride (fda approved for androgenic alopecia).

Safe for women with female pattern hair loss

No hormone changes in the serum of female patients undergoing injections.

Minimal adverse effects

There were no adverse effects and no alterations in serum hormones.
