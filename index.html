About the trial

There have been few new innovations in the hair loss industry. Preliminary scientific work has shown that dutasteride scalp injections result in the same efficacy as oral dutasteride with far fewer side-effects. We are currently working on obtaining FDA approval for intradermal dutasteride injections through the FDA 505(b)(2) pathway.

Safe for women with female pattern hair loss

No hormone changes in the serum of female patients undergoing injections.

100% effectiveness with no adverse effects or changes in serum hormones

6 patient study with injections with dutasteride 0.01% every 3 months showed an improvement in hair density in all patients. There were no adverse effects and no alterations in serum hormones
